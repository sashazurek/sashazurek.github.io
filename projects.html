BART Service Updates for Bluesky Social

As an early adopter of Bluesky and a regular BART user, I was missing easy access to the BART service alerts feed that's available on Formerly-Known-As-Twitter. I'm developing a bot which will automatically pull from BART's own RSS feed, format the alerts, and then post them on a regular interval by leveraging the ATProto API. It's currently fairly basic -- it polls the feed every 30 minutes and takes the topmost alert to post. In the future, I hope to add more frequent polling, duplication checking with a Supabase database, and the ability for Bluesky users to request specific line updates from the bot by tagging it in a post. The bot is built in Typescript.
